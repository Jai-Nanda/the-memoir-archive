Open Positions

The following positions are open for new members to join. Click here to apply!

Content Creator

Work with the content creation team. Produce at least 2 article every month. Attend team meetings every 2 weeks and try to suggest ideas for new article series, social media posts, etc.

Junior Editor

Verify the write-ups by the content team, fact-check the articles, and modify as needed. Attend team meetings every 2 weeks and work with the content creation team to suggest ideas for new article series, social media posts, etc.

Graphic Designer

Create posts for our social media pages. Discuss the ideas for the posts with a team of 2-3 content creators. Design weekly newsletter resources.

Web Developer

Keep the website running smoothly, troubleshoot any technical issues, post articles published by the editorial team, properly maintain pages, and improve search engine optimization.

Social Media Manager

Work on goal setting for social media, assist with creating, captioning, and posting relevant content across The Memoir Archive's Instagram (story + feed), Twitter, and Facebook, increasing the reach of social media pages, and getting shout outs on social media. Instagram/Facebook managers may be allowed to post informative reels and stories.

Outreach Manager

Reach out to different schools and organize events there. Organize events/collabs with other student organizations and NGOs in the form of article series, virtual events, promotions, etc
