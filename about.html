About Us

The Memoir archive is an organization by teenagers for teenagers seeking to empower teenagers worldwide with the resources and information necessary to be more inclusive and actively able to express themselves in better ways possible. Our goal is to create an active community and connect people with similar interests together and help them put their thoughts into words. We aim to develop a network of teens wherein they share their literary works with one another to enhance knowledge and enhance each other's personalities as well as to bring new information to the public.
