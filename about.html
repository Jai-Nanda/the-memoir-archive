About Us

The Memoir Archive is a student-found and student-managed organization to promote the notion of explanatory journalism among teens. We aim to spread knowledge about non-conventional topics which are not generally covered by educational institutes around the world. We are seeking to empower students worldwide with the resources and information necessary to explore more than what they are familiar with in their educational systems. Our goal is to reach out to different student communities who do not have access to information like this to embrace acceptance, redefine their perspectives positively, and create an active community.
